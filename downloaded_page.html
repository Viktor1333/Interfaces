Why Choose UACS?

85% of UACS students find employment following graduation.
UACS co-operates with universities that are ranked in the Top 500 Universities worldwide.
UACS is the only Internationally Accredited University in Macedonia.
UACS is supported by the Business Council which consists of more than 150 companies, organizations and institutions.
Our MBA program ranks among the Top 5 in Eastern Europe, and our International Double Degree Programs Rank in the Top 500 in the World.
UACS Graduate Programs are taught by Harvard Certified Professors.
